For those of you who are not very familiar with the definition of genome, here is the best answer. A genome is a full set of DNA coding sequence data for chromosomes: human genome contains 22 autonomes and two allosomes (i.e. X chromosome and Y chromosome), i.e. 24 chromosomes with 3,095,677,412 nucleotide base pairs; the chimpanzee genome contains 23 autonomes and a pair of allosomes with 3,323,267,922 nucleotide base pairs. The possibility of shared ancestor between human beings and chimpanzees are thoroughly disproved by so many  differences in the  DNA sequence data and Y chromosome, the most stable and unchangeable chromosome.

The false conclusion (only hypothetical) that Human Chromosome 2 is derived from merger of Chromosomes 2A and 2B of chimpanzees is often used as the basis of fusion. As is the case in other living creatures, the chromosome codes of chimpanzees shall be defined according to chromosome lengths. The longest one is coded as Chromosome 1, the second longest one is coded as Chromosome 2, and so forth. Why are there Chromosome 2A and 2B? It is because the proponents believe that these two chromosomes merged to become Human Chromosome 2.

They said with full certainty that by merging head- to-head, two chromosomes are close in length to the length of   human chromosome 2. In addition, they said that the genes on them matched closely to the gene arrangement on human chromosome 2. In the book The Language of God, the author used illustration to show the above points.

It is significantly different from the false conclusion they proposed before (but it is still false). Why? According to the new hypothesis, to merge to become   human chromosome 2, the two chimpanzee chromosomes (2A and 2B) have to abandon one third of their chromosome length. According to modern medical science, such an aberrant  chimpanzee cannot survive at all. It is even more impossible to create  both a man and a woman with completely identical chromosome sets.

In other words, a baby boy or baby girl with 23 pairs of chromosomes could be born in twenty five millions ancient man ape babies.

By probability, it takes two 50 thousand years or 100 thousand years to generate a new man and a new woman.
