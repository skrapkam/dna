GoodWatcher introduction


At present, there are many software products that provide surveillance function which the video/pictures to be broadcasted by the computer of the home site and to be watched by the computer of remote client sites through Internet.
However, the video quality is usually poor for this kind of products due to network speed limitations. Especially when the server sites (Home sites) that upload the images use DSL connection, and the client sites that download the images use a low speed network (such as 56 kbps/ sec.) Under these situations, regular software products often fail to function properly.
The unique approach used in GoodWatcher-105 enables it to work well under different network speeds. It can adjust the refresh rate of picture automatically according to the speed of the network and the image quality is always very good. In addition, the images are stored automatically in the computer of client sites for viewing at convinent times. Be sides being user friendly, it also provides many additional features such as Alarm with calling notifications and/or sending captured images to email boxes, the capability to remotely Arm/Disarm, or snap images.

The hardware and software requirements:
*Memory: >= 128MB
*Hard Drive: >= 6 Gigabytes
*Processors: >= 1.06 Gigahertz
*Operating System: Windows 2000/XP/VISTA
