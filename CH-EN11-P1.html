5. C value paradox, really?

 

۵ĴСӦͬڽĵλжӦĹϵȻֵCֵ󣬷ֲȫɡе㣬ṹ򵥣ڽ۵УҲ﷢չܵ͵ĵλCֵȴԶࡣǣоԱΪCֵ(Cvalue paradox)

 

According to the past biological concepts of evolution, the size of genome should be related to the position and complicacy of living beings in evolution. However, when the C values of more and more living beings are worked out, the above concept is found not completely true. Even some living creatures such as lungfish, with a simple body structure, have a very low position in biological development according to the Theory of Evolution, but they have a far higher C value than human beings. As a result of this kind of observation, biological researchers believe that the phenomenon is a C value paradox.

According to the above, the C value paradox affirms that all individuals of the same species have constant C value rather than refute the C value constancy theory. It believes that size ranking of C values of different living creatures does not confer to the rules of Theory of Evolution. According to opinion expressed in this Book, i.e. no evolution connection between species and According to its kind, the size of C value is not related to the degree of species evolution. Thus, this  is nota paradox. Why do living creatures of simple body structures have higher C values? Some scholar suggested, It hints that genomes of eukaryotes must contain a large quantity of nonprogrammed DNA sequences, which are very likely to control expression of genes, but no complete theory has been formed yet. (Reference 3) When some scientific facts are found in conflict with some theory, we shall analyze whether the theory is correct, which should lead to an attitude against the Theory of Evolution.

Therefore, it is impossible for human beings and chimpanzees to share one common ancestor.
