Who are we? Where are we from?


Who are we? Where are we from? These questions have troubled people for centuries. 


The textbooks have long taught us that human beings evolved from anthropoid apes. 


Today, a new science, molecular anthropology, tells us this: 7 billion human beings in the world share a common grand, grand…father, the Y-chromosome Adam, and share a common grand, grand…mother, the Mitochondrial Eve. After reading information in this book, you will be convinced that human beings could not be the result of evolution, sudden mutation, or in any way related to the anthropoid apes.
